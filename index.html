MUSTAFA KEMAL ATATÜRK

The Great Leader ↓

  • 1881 Mustafa was born in Thessaloniki.
  • 1893 Mustafa starts the Military Preparatory School in Thessaloniki, where he is given the middle name "Kemâl" by his teacher.
  • 1895 Mustafa Kemal starts the Military High School in Manastır.
  • 1899 Mustafa Kemal starts the preparatory class at the Harbiye in Istanbul.
  • 1902 Mustafa Kemal graduated from the Military Academy and then continued to the Military Academy.
  • January 1905 Mustafa Kemal graduated from the Military Academy as a Staff Captain and was sent to Damascus to serve in the Fifth Army in Damascus.
  • October 1906 Mustafa Kemal and his friends set up a secret society in Damascus under the name of "Vatan ve Hürriyet".
  • September 1907 Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the Third Army and sent to Thessaloniki.
  • September 1911 Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the General Staff in Istanbul.
  • January 1912 Mustafa Kemal successfully leads the Tobruk offensive in Libya.
  • November 1912 Mustafa Kemal is appointed to the Mediterranean Straits Special Forces as the Head of Action.
  • October 1913 Mustafa Kemal is appointed to Sofia as Military Attaché.
  • April 1915 The Allied Powers land in Arıburnu, and they stop their advance with the Mustafa Kemal Division.
  • August 1915 Mustafa Kemal is brought to Anafartalar Group Command.
  • April 1916 Mustafa Kemal is promoted to Brigadier General.
  • August 1916 Mustafa Kemal takes Bitlis and Muş back from the enemy.
  • October 1918 Mustafa Kemal becomes the Group Commander of the Yildirim Armies.
  • April 1919 Mustafa Kemal is appointed as an Inspector to the Ninth Army in Erzurum with wide powers.
  • 16 May 1919 Mustafa Kemal leaves Istanbul.
  • 19 May 1919 Mustafa Kemal reaches Samsun.
  • July 1919 Mustafa Kemal resigns from his position as Inspector of the Third Army and from the army.
  • July 1919 Mustafa Kemal is brought to the Presidency of Erzurum Congress.
  • September 1919 Mustafa Kemal is brought to the Presidency of Sivas Congress.
  • December 1919 Mustafa Kemal comes to Ankara with the Executive Committee.
  • April 23, 1920 Mustafa Kemal opens the Turkish Grand National Assembly in Ankara.
  • May 1920 Mustafa Kemal is sentenced to death by the Istanbul government.
  • August 1921 Mustafa Kemal is appointed by the Grand National Assembly as the Commander-in-Chief.
  • August 1921 The Battle of Sakarya, in which Turkish troops are led by Mustafa Kemal, begins.
  • September 1921 The TBMM gives Mustafa Kemal the rank of Marshal and the title of Gazi.
  • August 1922 Gazi Mustafa Kemal begins to lead the Great Offensive from Kocatepe.
  • August 1922 Gazi Mustafa Kemal Pasha wins the battle of Dumlupınar.
  • September 1922 Gazi Mustafa Kemal enters Izmir.
  • November 1922 The Grand National Assembly of Turkey accepts Gazi Mustafa Kemal's proposal to abolish the Caliphate.
  • January 1923 Mustafa Kemal's mother, Zübeyde Hanım, passed away in İzmir.
  • October 29, 1923 The Republic of Turkey is proclaimed and Gazi Mustafa Kemal is elected as the first President.
  • August 1924 Gazi Mustafa Kemal wears a hat for the first time in Istanbul Sarayburnu.
  • August 1928 Gazi Mustafa Kemal gives a speech on the new Turkish Alphabet in Sarayburnu.
  • April 12, 1931 Gazi Mustafa Kemal establishes the Turkish Historical Society.
  • July 12, 1932Gazi Mustafa Kemal establishes the Turkish Language Institution.
  • June 16, 1934 The Turkish Grand National Assembly gave Gazi Mustafa Kemal the surname "Atatürk".
  • November 10, 1938Gazi Mustafa Kemal Atatürk passed away.
